Booking Information
How it works:
  1. Select a parking lot from the available options
  2. Click "Book Spot" to get the next available spot
  3. You'll be automatically assigned a spot number
  4. Park your vehicle and the timer starts
  5. Come back to release the spot when leaving
Pricing:

You'll be charged based on the hourly rate of the selected parking lot. The cost is calculated from the time you book until you release the spot.

You can only have one active parking reservation at a time.
